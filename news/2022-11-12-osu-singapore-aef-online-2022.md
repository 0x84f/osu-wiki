Hot off the heels of DreamJam (as recently featured in osu!monthly), the osu! Singapore community is back for more with the third iteration of GST:LIVE!

![GST:LIVE 2022 Banner](/wiki/shared/news/2022-11-12-osu-singapore-aef-online-2022/banner.png)

This year, the Singapore LAN tournament will be held as part of [Asia Esports Festival Online 2022](https://esportsasia.net), held at Kallang Wave Mall, Singapore on 17th December.

If you're in Singapore next month and hungry for competition, head on over to the [GST:LIVE website](https://gstlive.org) to sign up now!

## Tournament

GST:LIVE is an open 2v2 tournament with its qualifiers stage held online, with the top eight qualifying teams advancing to play off in the bracket stage offline on 17th December, held within the venue.

All 29 beatmaps throughout the 3 mappools will be custom-mapped, along with 2 custom songs that will be revealed in time.

For more information about the tournament, refer to this [wiki article](/wiki/Tournaments/GSTLIVE/22).
