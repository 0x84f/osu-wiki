The first ever "skin of the year" contest has concluded in a spectacular down-to-the-wire fashion! Tune in to see the results!

![](/wiki/shared/news/2023-03-05-skin-of-the-year-2022-results/banner.jpg)

After a gruelling 2 week-long battle that had the top 2 skins being neck in neck for 1st place until the very end, the dust has settled, the votes were locked in, and a winner was chosen. After all... there can be only one!

---

![](/wiki/shared/news/2023-03-05-skin-of-the-year-2022-results/If-there-was-an-endpoint.jpg)

With a mind-blowing **1208** votes, [if there was an endpoint.](https://osu.ppy.sh/community/forums/topics/1672096) by [- Koshiin -](https://osu.ppy.sh/users/19135423) won the hearts and minds of people with beautiful art resembling the band "TUYU", which this skin is dedicated to. Congratulations!

![](BADGE)

![](/wiki/shared/news/2023-03-05-skin-of-the-year-2022-results/Moonshine.jpg)

If someone were to tell you that all that was separating [Moonshine 2.0](https://osu.ppy.sh/community/forums/topics/1610388) from securing a 1st place spot was 27 votes, would you believe them? Well believe it or not, [moonstaar](https://osu.ppy.sh/users/27339355) has achieved 2nd place with an amazing **1181** votes with their minimalistic, yet ever so satisfying entry!

![](BADGE)

![](/wiki/shared/news/2023-03-05-skin-of-the-year-2022-results/Shiroko-Swimsuit.jpg)

Anime girls and skins, name me a more iconic duo... I'll wait.
**1054** voters loved this specific duo as [BatuhanYtho](https://osu.ppy.sh/users/12091015)'s [Shiroko Swimsuit](https://osu.ppy.sh/community/forums/topics/1641543?n=1) skin combines a clean skin optimised for gameplay with Shiroko from *Blue Archive* in a swimsuit, watching your every move.

![](BADGE)

![](/wiki/shared/news/2023-03-05-skin-of-the-year-2022-results/Wintherest.jpg)

For people with a habit of searching for high quality original skins on the forums, seeing [Redo_](https://osu.ppy.sh/users/7122165)'s name pop definitely rings a bell! Their [- 『Wintherest』 -](https://osu.ppy.sh/community/forums/topics/1498493) entry achieved 1st place during the ["Tides of Winter" skinning contest](/wiki/Contests/Skinning_Contest/2) a year ago, and still holds up with an impressive 4th place this time around with **932** votes! If you are an enjoyer of Winter you will definitely enjoy this entry.

![](/wiki/shared/news/2023-03-05-skin-of-the-year-2022-results/Hitori-Gotou.jpg)

Do you like Bocchi the Rock? Do you like the colour pink? Then [Hitori Gotou](https://osu.ppy.sh/community/forums/topics/1695565?n=1) is for you! [Hoshinomori](https://osu.ppy.sh/users/25025133) was able to secure themselves the 5th spot in this contest with **886** votes, all of them being from Bocchi the Rock enjoyers.

![](/wiki/shared/news/2023-03-05-skin-of-the-year-2022-results/Whatsapp.jpg)

**795** members of the WhatsApp gang showed up for the voting while blasting the WhatsApp trap remix, and cast their votes for [XetThe](https://osu.ppy.sh/users/16274977)'s [WhatsApp skin](https://osu.ppy.sh/community/forums/topics/1521212), which netted them the 6th place. All jokes aside, XetThe took a gimmick and made something actually pleasing to look at and play with.

![](/wiki/shared/news/2023-03-05-skin-of-the-year-2022-results/Soumetsu.jpg)

Another previous contest winner has made their way into the top 10 skins of 2022, so feel free to congratulate [thetasigma](https://osu.ppy.sh/users/6234482) on their **765** votes and 7th place! [霜滅 SOUMETSU](https://osu.ppy.sh/community/forums/topics/1628514?n=1) features Ayaka from *Genshin Impact* while only using the Protanopia colour spectrum from the [Chromatic Alteration skinning contest](/wiki/Contests/Skinning_Contest/3), the result of which is a high quality skin you can use for both gameplay and for looks.

![](/wiki/shared/news/2023-03-05-skin-of-the-year-2022-results/The-Memory-of-Mumei.jpg)

What, this guy again? Not only did [thetasigma](https://osu.ppy.sh/users/6234482) win 2 out of the top 10 places, they also managed to get their 2 entries voted right next to each other! [The Memory of Mumei](https://osu.ppy.sh/community/forums/topics/1569498) gives off a brass, steampunk vibe with beautiful drawings by several artists, which is why **753** community members deemed this skin worthy of their vote.

![](/wiki/shared/news/2023-03-05-skin-of-the-year-2022-results/Cosmos.jpg)

Are you a fan of space? Do you enjoy simple, yet detailed skins? Then [RUDEKA](https://osu.ppy.sh/users/13015586)'s [Cosmos](https://osu.ppy.sh/community/forums/topics/1586852?n=1) is for you! With a soothing blue tone being present, along with depictions of the cosmos displayed across the skin, it is easy to how this entry gained **747** votes.

![](/wiki/shared/news/2023-03-05-skin-of-the-year-2022-results/Saggitarius-A.jpg)

Last, but definitely not the least, we have [Sagittarius A](https://osu.ppy.sh/community/forums/topics/1683356?n=1), the 2nd entry from [BatuhanYtho](https://osu.ppy.sh/users/12091015) that made the top 10. The aforementioned fans of space can rejoice once more, as another space themed skin grazes the top 10. Gorgeous shades of purple are ever-present, with black holes being a mainstay throughout the entire skin.

---

The top 3 entries will all receive a unique profile badge based on their entry along with 4, 3 and 2 months of osu!supporter respectively, and entries #4-#10 will all receive a month of osu!supporter each.

If you are curious about the full results for all entries, or just want to see where your favourite skins placed, you can find them over on the [contest listing](https://osu.ppy.sh/community/contests/170).

2022 has been a slower year for skinning in terms of skins being submitted, however the quality has kept up and went even higher than previous years, so we want to thank everyone who participate within the skinning community, whether it'd be creation or just providing nice feedback, we are glad to have you here!
