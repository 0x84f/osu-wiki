And your osu!taiko World Cup champion is...

![](/wiki/Tournaments/TWC/2024/img/twc2024-banner.jpg)

Japan. It's Japan. By now it has become a meme that the osu!taiko World Cup champion is known even before the tournament starts, but Japan have earned that reputation fair and square. Shedding blood and tears every year in order to maintain their dominance isn't an easy task, and for the 10th year in a row they managed to uphold their 1st place on the podium.

## Summary

![](/wiki/Tournaments/TWC/2024/img/twc2024-podium.jpg)

Congratulations to Japan for securing their 10th consecutive osu!taiko World Cup victory, and congratulations to the United Kingdom and United States on their 2nd and 3rd podium spots respectively!

While there isn't much to say about Japan for the 10th year in a row, an interesting thing to note about these podium placements is that this is the first time ever that the podium is shared by 3 different continents!
