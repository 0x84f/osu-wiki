# Off-topic (disambiguation)

**Off-topic** can have multiple meanings:

- [Chat](/wiki/Internet_Relay_Chat) or [forum](/wiki/Community/Forum) messages that don't follow the ongoing discussion's theme.
- [Off-Topic](/wiki/Community/Forum/Off-Topic), an osu! forum for random discussions that don't belong to other forums.
