# Score Multiplier

*For other uses, see [Score Multiplier (disambiguation)](/wiki/Disambiguation/Score_multiplier).*

**Score Multiplier** refers to the combination of the [combo multiplier](/wiki/Gameplay/Combo_(score_multiplier)), the [mod multiplier](/wiki/Gameplay/Game_modifier/Score_multiplier(mod_multiplier)) and the [difficulty multiplier](/wiki/Gameplay/Score/ScoreV1/osu!#Difficulty_multiplier). By using a formula, it directly determines a player's score throughout a play.
