# Rhythm

**Rhythm** is the pattern of sound, silence, and emphasis in a song.[[1]][r] In osu!, mappers utilise these combinations when creating beatmaps; notes are represented by [beats](/wiki/Music_theory/Beat) and accents are represented by [hitsounds](/wiki/Beatmapping/Hitsound).
